Week3 Guest Speaker

Our guest speaker today was Alison Letjemeier from Adobe. She is the director of Customer Feedback & Voice of the Customer (VOC). She has worked in tech for many years. My biggest take away from her was that you need to network. Also make sure you are working and growing in ways to progress into the job that you want next. When opportunities are given to you take them. We as women tend to not take a job because we feel underqualified for the job. It is okay to reach out for those jobs that you are mostly qualified for, most employers are more than happy to help train you in the areas that you feel deficient.

Week 5 Making Wireframes Reality and Tara Shipp (Oct. 14th)

Week 5 Guest Speaker

Today we heard from Tara Shipp a UX/UI Designer. One of the first things that Tara shared with us is that Good UX is useful, usable, and delightful. I have a very difficult time making things pretty and useful. I don't like design I would much rather test the product and give feedback. Tara shared a little test with us about logging into a program and it not allowing you to enter because your username or password is incorrect. This can be frustrating becuase you don't know which was incorrect. UX can help the end user know which is incorrect thus making it less frustrating. It was fun to try to troubleshoot how you would solve the problem and hear others input on how to solve the problem.
